Released [PaperZorro](https://rafael-silva-oliveira.github.io/projects/paperzorro/), an AI-powered literature scout for Zotero. Build precise boolean searches across PubMed, arXiv and bioRxiv/medRxiv, let a language model filter the noise, and import only the papers that matter — with scheduled searches that keep your library current automatically.
