Published a first release of [NZBridge](https://rafael-silva-oliveira.github.io/projects/nzbridge/), a browser extension and Zotero plugin for bidirectional sync between Zotero and Google NotebookLM. Push your research library into NotebookLM for AI-powered analysis, then pull generated notes back into Zotero.
